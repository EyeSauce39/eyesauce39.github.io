Our Story

Founded in late 2018, RealFiction was created by LittleNicholas and EyeSauce39, and began as a factions server for their friends to play on. The original RealFiction focused on Factions gamemode, but had other gamemodes like Bedwars, and RF originals such as, Vault and Tournaments. The first factions world started all the way back in 2018, now stands as a museum piece for admiration and reminiscence. RealFiction had many ups and downs throughout the years, but was officially revamped in early 2024 with a brand new factions world, new spawn, and new gamemodes. What makes RealFiction unique is our inclusivity and community-centric mindset that still stands firmly against Pay-To-Win game style, or staff toxicity.

Gamemodes

Factions

Factions is a survival-style gamemode, but with an added twist! Factions allows you to create, manage, and grow your own kingdom, fight with other kingdoms, or form alliances. Other aspects include being able to sell your goods to other factions and perform raids.

Tournaments

An exclusive gamemode to RealFiction, Tournaments combines elements of Champions gamemode and Hunger Games. You spawn on a map with up to 8 total people and fight to the death. You get multiple lives, and whoever gets the most kills wins!

Bedwars

Defend your base! Bedwars is a PVP gamemode where 4 teams of differing colors have to protect their teams block, keep outsiders away, and try and steal the block from other teams.

Creative Plots

Creative plots allow you to have your own plot(s) where you're free to build whatever you choose, and show off your creations!

Parkour

Parkour is a game mode in Minecraft that tests players' agility by requiring them to run, jump, and perform other acrobatic moves to get from one point to another as quickly as possible
