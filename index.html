Who We Are

Founded in late 2018, RealFiction SMP is a fun, community-driven Minecraft server designed for players who value fair gameplay and a welcoming environment. We specialize in Survival and Factions, with plenty of other gamemodes to keep the adventure going. Our server is proudly anti pay-to-win, ensuring a level playing field for everyone. Whether you're teaming up with friends or carving out your own path, RealFiction SMP offers the perfect mix of creativity, challenge, and community spirit.
