Rules

General

  • - Keep chat appropriate: Use appropriate language and refrain from any form of offensive, vulgar, or inappropriate communication.
  • - Respect other players: Treat fellow players with kindness and respect. No harassment, discrimination, or bullying will be tolerated.
  • - Any advertisement of scams or deceptive services is strictly prohibited
  • - Report issues responsibly: If you encounter bugs, glitches, or rule violations, report them to staff members rather than exploiting them or taking matters into your own hand
  • - Any form of harassment is not allowed
  • - Asking for staff, gamemode switches, or cheated items is prohibited
  • - No spamming or advertising: Avoid excessive messaging, advertising other servers, or promoting unrelated content.
  • - No excessive redstone: Avoid creating lag-inducing redstone contraptions or excessively complex machinery that may impact server performance.
